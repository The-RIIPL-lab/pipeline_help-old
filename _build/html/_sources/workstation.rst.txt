RIIPL Workstations
==================

The **RIIPL** Lab is located on the second floor of the MRI Building at the Wake Forest Baptist Hospital campus on Queen Street
in Winston-Salem, North Carolina.

We offer a lab space with workstations equipped with common fMRI software toolkits, such as:

* Matlab, with the following toolkits:
	* Statistical Parametric Mapping (SPM)
	* Voxel-Based Morphometry (VBM)
	* Computational Anatomy Toolbox for SPM (CAT)
	* Lesion Segmentation Tookkit (LST)
	* Neurite Orientation Dispersion and Density Imaging (NODDI) toolkit
	* Brain Connectivity Toolbox (BCT)
* FSL
* FreeSurfer
* AFNI
* ANTs
* ITK
* VTK
