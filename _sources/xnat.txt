.. _xnat:

Using RIIPL XNAT
================

The RIIPL laboratory uses **XNAT** to upload, receive, and manage images.

What is XNAT?
^^^^^^^^^^^^^

`XNAT <https://www.xnat.org/>`_ is an open-source imaging informatics software platform. 
Most of your interactions with the RIIPL system will occur through our XNAT installation.
