Welcome to the RIIPL Codex
==========================

**RIIPL** is the Radiology Informatics & Image Processing Laboratory at Wake Forest University Baptist Medical Center.
It is designed to automate the processing and analysis of neurological images, facilitate secure sharing of information, and provide access to common fMRI research tools.
